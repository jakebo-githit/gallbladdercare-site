🌾 止腹泻ANTI-DIARRHEA 医生推荐Dr. Pick
🩺 适用症状：术后腹泻 / 大便稀溏 🩺 For: post-op diarrhea / loose stools

洋车前子壳纤维粉（Psyllium Husk） Psyllium Husk Fiber Powder

胆囊切除后，胆汁失去储存机制，转为持续滴入肠道。当过多的游离胆酸进入结肠，会刺激肠壁加速蠕动，引发"胆酸性腹泻"（Bile Acid Diarrhea）——这是术后腹泻最常见的原因，却经常被忽视。洋车前子壳是一种可溶性膳食纤维，在肠道中形成黏性凝胶，能有效吸附多余胆酸，从源头缓解腹泻。每日一至两次，随餐服用，通常数天内即可见效。 After cholecystectomy, bile continuously trickles into the intestine without the gallbladder's storage and concentration function. Excess free bile acids entering the colon stimulate bowel wall motility, causing Bile Acid Diarrhea (BAD) — the most common yet most frequently overlooked cause of post-op diarrhea. Psyllium husk is a soluble fiber that forms a viscous gel in the gut, binding excess bile acids at the source. Taken once or twice daily with meals, most patients notice improvement within a few days.

  • ✔ 解决根本原因（胆酸过多），而非只掩盖症状✔ Addresses root cause (excess bile acids), not just symptoms
  • ✔ 同时改善肠道规律性，防止便秘与腹泻交替✔ Also regulates bowel regularity — prevents constipation/diarrhea cycling
  • ✔ 天然、非处方、无成瘾性，适合长期使用✔ Natural, OTC, non-habit-forming — suitable for long-term use

🦠 补益生菌PROBIOTICS 医生推荐Dr. Pick
🩺 适用症状：菌群失调 / 腹胀 / 消化不规律 🩺 For: gut dysbiosis / bloating / irregular digestion

优质益生菌胶囊（含 LGG 菌株） High-Quality Probiotic (Lactobacillus rhamnosus GG)

手术本身以及围手术期抗生素的使用，会对肠道菌群造成显著冲击。许多患者在术后数周内出现持续腹胀、排气增多、大便不成形，很大程度上源于菌群失衡。鼠李糖乳杆菌 GG（LGG）是目前临床证据最充分的益生菌菌株之一，已在数十项随机对照试验中被证实能够有效恢复肠道微生态、缩短抗生素相关性腹泻病程、改善术后消化功能。选择含 LGG 的产品时，请确认菌落数（CFU）在 100 亿以上，且有肠溶包衣保护。 Surgery itself and perioperative antibiotic use deal a significant blow to the gut microbiome. Many patients experience weeks of persistent bloating, excess gas, and loose stools post-surgery — largely driven by microbial imbalance. Lactobacillus rhamnosus GG (LGG) has the strongest clinical evidence base among probiotic strains, validated across dozens of randomized controlled trials for restoring gut microbiota, shortening antibiotic-associated diarrhea, and improving post-operative digestive function. When selecting an LGG product, confirm at least 10 billion CFU with enteric coating for acid protection.

  • ✔ 改善腹胀、排气过多、大便不成形等常见术后症状✔ Reduces bloating, excess gas, and soft stools common after surgery

🩺 适用症状：油腻不耐受 / 进食后腹胀 / 大便颜色变浅 🩺 For: fat intolerance / post-meal bloating / pale stools

消化酶复合配方（含牛胆汁提取物 + 脂肪酶） Digestive Enzyme Complex with Ox Bile Extract + Lipase

胆囊的核心功能是浓缩并储存肝脏分泌的胆汁，进餐时集中释放——这个"定时爆发"对脂肪乳化至关重要。胆囊切除后，胆汁变为持续低浓度涓滴式分泌，遇到高脂饮食时乳化能力明显不足，常出现进食后腹胀、油腻不耐受，严重者甚至大便颜色变浅（脂肪泻）。含牛胆汁提取物（Ox Bile Extract）和高活性脂肪酶（Lipase）的消化酶补剂，能从体外补充这部分缺失的乳化与消化功能，帮助脂溶性营养素（维生素 A、D、E、K）的吸收，改善进餐后的舒适度。 The gallbladder's core function is to concentrate and store liver-produced bile, releasing it in a concentrated burst when needed to emulsify dietary fat. After removal, bile trickles at low concentration throughout the day — insufficient for fatty meals, leading to post-meal bloating, fat intolerance, and in some cases, pale or greasy stools (steatorrhea). A digestive enzyme supplement containing Ox Bile Extract and high-activity Lipase externally restores this missing emulsification capacity, supports absorption of fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K), and significantly improves post-meal comfort.
