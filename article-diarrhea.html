🌾 第一篇 · 腹泻机制Article 1 · Diarrhea Mechanism

胆囊切除术后，为什么还在腹泻？
一个被反复忽视的答案 Why Are You Still Having Diarrhea After Gallbladder Removal? An Answer That's Too Often Missed.

发生率约30%，经常被漏诊为肠易激综合征——真正的原因叫"胆酸性腹泻（BAD）"。 Affecting ~30% of patients, frequently misdiagnosed as IBS-D — the real cause is called Bile Acid Diarrhea (BAD).

在我接诊的术后患者里，腹泻是被投诉最多、也是被忽视最多的症状。很多人在手术后数周、甚至数月里，每天大便3到5次，稀溏不成形，尤其在进食后加重。他们去复查，被告知"手术很成功"，然后被嘱咐"少吃油"。但这个答案是不够的。 In my post-operative consultations, diarrhea is the most frequently complained about — and most frequently dismissed — symptom. Many patients experience 3 to 5 loose bowel movements daily for weeks or even months after surgery, worsening after meals. They return for follow-up and are told "the surgery was successful" and advised to "eat less fat." That answer isn't enough.

真正的原因：胆酸性腹泻（BAD） The Real Cause: Bile Acid Diarrhea (BAD)

正常情况下，胆囊的功能是储存并浓缩胆汁，在你进餐时感知到脂肪，才集中释放——这叫"按需供胆"。胆囊切除后，这套调节机制消失了。肝脏仍在持续分泌胆汁，但没有胆囊储存，胆汁就转变成持续的低流量"涓滴"，24小时不间断地流入小肠。 Under normal conditions, the gallbladder stores and concentrates bile, releasing it in a coordinated burst when it senses fat arriving — an on-demand system. After cholecystectomy, this regulatory mechanism is gone. The liver continues secreting bile, but without a gallbladder to store it, bile trickles continuously at low concentration into the small intestine, 24 hours a day.

问题出在这里：当胆汁量超过小肠的吸收能力，多余的游离胆酸就会进入结肠。结肠对胆酸非常敏感——胆酸会刺激结肠分泌水分、加速肠蠕动，结果就是水样腹泻或稀便。这就是临床上所说的"胆酸性腹泻（Bile Acid Diarrhea，BAD）"，在胆囊切除患者中发生率约为25%至35%，是术后腹泻最常见的原因。 Here's the problem: when bile exceeds the small intestine's absorption capacity, excess free bile acids spill into the colon. The colon is highly sensitive to bile acids — they trigger colonic secretion of water and accelerate motility, resulting in watery or loose stools. This is clinically known as Bile Acid Diarrhea (BAD), occurring in approximately 25–35% of cholecystectomy patients, making it the most common cause of post-operative diarrhea.

为什么医生经常漏诊它？ Why Is It So Often Missed?

胆酸性腹泻在普通内科门诊中容易被误诊为肠易激综合征（IBS-D）。两者症状高度相似——都表现为餐后腹泻、腹痛缓解后腹泻，都在紧张时加重。区别在于：BAD 的患者通常有明确的胆囊手术史，而且症状往往在进食高脂食物（如炒菜、肉类）后更明显。黄金诊断标准是 SeHCAT 核素扫描，但这项检查在很多医院并不常规开展。 Bile Acid Diarrhea is frequently misdiagnosed as IBS-D (diarrhea-predominant irritable bowel syndrome) in general outpatient settings. The two conditions share nearly identical symptoms — post-meal diarrhea, abdominal pain relieved by defecation, worsening with stress. The key difference: BAD patients typically have a clear cholecystectomy history, and symptoms are more pronounced after higher-fat meals. The gold-standard diagnosis is the SeHCAT nuclear scan, but this test is not routinely performed in most hospitals.

洋车前子壳：为什么是正确的解决方案 Psyllium Husk: Why It's the Right Solution

很多患者第一反应是吃止泻药（洛哌丁胺，即 Imodium）。这不能说完全错，但它只是症状控制，没有解决根本问题。正确的方向是减少进入结肠的游离胆酸量。洋车前子壳（Psyllium Husk）是一种可溶性膳食纤维，在肠道遇水后形成黏性凝胶——这层凝胶能与胆酸结合，将其截留在肠腔内，随粪便排出，而不是让胆酸自由刺激结肠壁。 Many patients' first instinct is to take anti-diarrheal medication (loperamide / Imodium). This isn't entirely wrong, but it only controls the symptom — it doesn't address the root cause. The correct approach is to reduce the amount of free bile acids reaching the colon. Psyllium husk is a soluble fiber that, when hydrated in the gut, forms a viscous gel. This gel binds bile acids, trapping them within the intestinal lumen and carrying them out with stool — rather than allowing free bile acids to irritate the colon wall.
