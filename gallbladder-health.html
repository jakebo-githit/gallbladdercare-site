术后为什么身体变了？——三个你必须读懂的机制 Why Did Your Body Change After Surgery? — 3 Mechanisms You Need to Understand

Here, in plain evidence-based language, we explain the three most common post-surgical challenges.

为什么还在腹泻？ Why Are You Still Having Diarrhea After Surgery?

每天腹泻3到5次，医生说"手术成功了"——但没人告诉你真正的原因是什么。答案叫"胆酸性腹泻（BAD）"，发生率约30%，却经常被漏诊。 3–5 loose stools daily, doctor says "surgery was successful" — but no one explains why. The answer is Bile Acid Diarrhea (BAD), affecting ~30% of patients, yet frequently missed.

⚗️ 第三篇 · 消化酶Article 3 · Digestive Enzymes

没有胆囊，
脂肪消化会发生什么？ What Happens to Fat Digestion
Without a Gallbladder?

胆囊能把胆汁浓缩5至10倍，切除后这个机制消失了。光靠"少吃油"是不够的——还有维生素 A、D、E、K 的吸收问题。 The gallbladder concentrates bile 5–10x. After removal, that mechanism is gone. "Eating less fat" isn't enough — vitamins A, D, E, K absorption is also affected.
